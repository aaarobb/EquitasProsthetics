Our Purpose

Equitas Prosthetics is a student-led initiative dedicated to bridging the gap in prosthetic accessibility. With today's exuberant market prices for the supposed "luxury" of basic mobility, we leverage 3D-printing technology to create mechanical devices, thus bypassing traditional financial obstacles. We believe mobility is a right, and through affordable innovation and compassion, we are building a world where everyone can regain their independence. And as such, we strive to continue innovating.
